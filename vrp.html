I created a website for anyone who is interested in learning about the history, parts and awesome features the record player has to offer. I used a variety of resources from Youtube to other audio playing websites along with codepen to help me get started with making the record player.

I created VRP because I wanted an online source that could resemble a record player for those who may not know very much about them. The focus of the brief was to create a website for an object, so I created a parts page, a listen page, and a story page that explains everything you need to know about turn- tables.
