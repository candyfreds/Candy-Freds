Lucid

    Key Words

  • Lucid
  • Engaging
  • Colorful
  • Unique

Lucid is a publication dedicated to women identifying type designers who use their knowledge of the world and graphic design to create unique, legible, and artistic typefaces. The three main artists featured in this magazine are Sofie Beier, Carol Twombly, and Emilie Riguad.

This publication focuses on the theme “lucid” which means to express clearly and to understand easily. My team wanted to engage the reader while also juxtaposing this idea of being legible. We found that high contrasting colors along with more organic shapes surrounding the variety of typefaces gave the book an interesting and exciting style.
