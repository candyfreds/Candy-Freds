Flow - Meditation App

    Key Words

  • Meditation
  • Calm
  • Sef Improvement
  • Health

Flow is a Meditation App that focuses on improving mood management with elements of logging and scheduling. As the user flows through the app, the interface recognizes your feelings and adapts its content based on changing moods.

There are two main sections of this app which include the meditation section and music section. You can explore through different sessions, exercises, and music to find exactly what you're looking for, all followed by a visual mood logging process.
