Greenway Arts Alliance in association with Da Poetry Lounge presents the 3rd Annual LA Get Down Festival, celebrating spoken word and hip-hop. The festival takes place from April 18-29 during National Poetry Month featuring local poets, artists, and tons of events/workshops. Speak Up aims to represent and reflect the unheard voices and rich diversity of Los Angeles.

The branding of this event highlights the passion and creativity found in speaking up and letting our voices be heard. Speak Up intends to excite the public about spoken word and hip-hop using bold typography, contrasting colors and visual cadence. This is the chance for individuals to express themselves through different mediums and further develop their voice!
