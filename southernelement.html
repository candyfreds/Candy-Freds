Southern Element is a cosmetic brand that was inspired from the red rocks and nature based elements surrounding the southern region of Utah. I wanted to focus on my type design in order to keep the packaging simple yet eye catching. The organic lines, patterns, and colors were taken directly from the scenery in different areas of Moab, and inspired from layers of sedimentary rock.

I used the main chemical compounds found in local rocks including Limestone (Si04), Charcoal (CaC03), and Sandstone (Si02) as the titles for each product. The audience for this brand includes adventurists who are looking for natural, cruality free soap that is both cheap yet reliable.
