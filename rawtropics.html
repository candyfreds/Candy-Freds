Raw Tropics

    Key Words

  • Fresh
  • Organic
  • Playful
  • Versatile

Raw Tropics is an organic coconut water brand that strives to hydrate individuals who are interested in outdoor activities, working out, or just lounging around on a hot summers day. With all natural flavors, Raw Tropics is a healthier alternative to other coconut waters because it contains no additives or sugar. This allows for a more natural flavor to come through and gives the consumer a cleaner and healthier source of hydration. I wanted to create a beach vibe that grasped the viewers attention by using bright tropical colors and patterns, as well as a variety of flavors that look and taste great together.

My word mark changed significantly from the beggining of the process, and turned out looking clean, proffesional, and closley related to the product itself: coconut water. I made a more round typeface for the word “RAW” and ended up choosing an organic, yet crisp typeface for the “TROPICS” which helped juxtapose the playful patterns and colors in the background.
