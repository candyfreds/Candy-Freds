Peach Pit Album

    Key Words

  • Indie
  • Pyschodelic
  • Contrast
  • Simple

Canadian band from Vancouver, British Columbia. The current line-up consists of bassist Peter Wilton, guitarist Christopher Vanderkooy, drummer Mikey Pascuzzi, and vocalist/guitarist Neil Smith. The primary audience for the Peach Pit album would be young adults or teens. However, this album was designed for anyone interested in buying a cool record set or listening to Peach Pit.

I wanted to focus on a dynamic album cover layout that not only folds apart to create one big picture on the inside, but also captures the relaxing vibes of Peach Pit. I’ve been playing around with saturation as well as contrast to give the album a unique look that is psychodelic but simple.
