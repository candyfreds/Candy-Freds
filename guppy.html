A Guppies Guide To Scuba Diving

    Key Words

  • Passion
  • Voice
  • Creativity
  • Bold

A Guppies Guide to Scuba Diving is an illustrated booklet that follows a character throughout the process of putting on different types of scuba gear. My initial idea consisted of turning the research and information into an interactive pamphlet for anyone to learn a few quick facts about scuba diving. The more I started researching, the more information came to the surface which lead me to design a longer booklet. My illustration style was inspired by flat, grainy, cut out cartoon characters from tv shows. This style is easy to visualize and separate detailed parts of color on the pages.

Although I did not intend for this project to be a kids book, I wanted an easy, non-threatening or scary way of displaying information on a topic that is not very common. I also think it is important for kids to have a basic understanding of scuba when they are young so they can be safe when diving in the future.
