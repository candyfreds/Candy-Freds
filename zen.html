I created Zen Dictionary for anyone who is interested in learning about meditation, yoga, health, and exercise from the comfort of their own home. I used a variety of resources from Youtube to other meditation websites in order to have everything in one place. Zen Dictionary was inspired by being in quarantine during this time as well as for after when people just want to learn the basics of health without the stress of going to classes or other events they may not be comfortable with.

Some of my successes were basic type hierarchy along with an easy to navigate nav bar. I wanted the site to be simple enough for anyone to use while also giving it dynamic elements that make the site less static. I also succeeded using many different jquery and javascript functions along with playing around with media queries so people can use the site on their computer at home or on their phones if they are out and about.
