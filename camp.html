Rock ’n’ Roll Camp for Girls is a non-profit that amplifies the voices of girls ages 9-17 who are cis-female, transgender (regardless of identity), and gender non-binary campers as they form bands, and perform together live before an audience. We provide a fun, fearless, and supportive environment for girls explore music, art, and creative self-expression.

This is an interactive site I designed for Rock 'n' Roll Camp for Girls in Portland, OR. My concept includes a simple yet fun way to learn information while playing a digital instrument. I experimented with scripting svg files with the use of audio editing and inverted hover effects.
