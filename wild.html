Wild Diversity is a non-profit organization that helps to create a personal connection to the outdoors for Black, Indigenous, all People of Color (BIPOC) & the LGBTQ+ communities, through outdoor adventures and education. This re-brand was inspired by the theme "golden hour" which is a period of daytime shortly after sunrise or before sunset.

Me and two classmates including Nika Kaiser and Harleigh Roberts had the opportunity to help Wild Diversity with their brand identity including logo, color scheme, social media content, swag, etc. This was an amazing experience that helped with our knowledge of real world experience and team/communication skills.
